BRIGHT AS A CREATOR

Co-founder of Light and Presence Beyond the Mic, a faith-based podcast inspiring believers to walk in the light and presence of the Lord.

Light and Presence Beyond the Mic

Bright is the co-founder of Light and Presence Beyond the Mic, a faith-based podcast that exists in two formats: audio content on Spotify and animated video content on YouTube. This creative endeavor was inspired by the powerful scripture 1 John 1:5-7, which speaks to walking in the light and the presence of the Lord.

Our Mission

The podcast's aim is to encourage believers and Christians to walk in the light and presence of the Lord at all times, all to the glory of God and God alone. Through thoughtful discussions, biblical insights, and spiritual encouragement, the podcast seeks to inspire listeners and viewers to deepen their relationship with God and live out their faith authentically in their daily lives.

Scriptural Foundation

The podcast draws its inspiration from 1 John 1:5-7, which reminds us that God is the source of all light and holiness, and that believers should walk in the light by living in a way that is consistent with God's character. This involves being in genuine fellowship with God and other believers, which is only possible through the cleansing blood of Jesus that atones for our sins.

Multi-Platform Presence

Recognizing that people consume content in different ways, Light and Presence Beyond the Mic is available as audio podcasts on Spotify for those who prefer to listen on the go, and as animated videos on YouTube for those who enjoy visual storytelling. This dual approach allows the podcast to reach a broader audience and meet people where they are in their spiritual journey.
